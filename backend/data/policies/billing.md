# Billing and Payment Policy - POL-003

## Payment Methods

**Accepted:** 
- All major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards with credit card logo
- Marriott Bonvoy points (full or partial redemption)
- Cash (for incidentals only, not for room charges)

**Authorization Hold:** A hold equal to the total room cost plus $100 per night for incidentals is placed on the credit card at check-in.

## Cancellation Policy

**Standard Rate:** 
- Cancel by 6:00 PM on day of arrival for full refund
- Cancellations after 6:00 PM or no-shows: charged one night's rate

**Non-Refundable Rates:** 
- No cancellations or changes allowed
- No refund for early departure

**Elite Members:** 
- Titanium and Ambassador Elite: Flexible cancellation up to 48 hours before arrival, even on non-refundable rates

## Resort Fees

**Daily Resort Fee:** $35 per room, per night (where applicable)

**Includes:**
- High-speed Wi-Fi
- Fitness center access
- Pool access
- Business center usage
- Local phone calls
- Daily newspaper

## Disputes

**Timeline:** Billing disputes must be filed within 30 days of checkout.

**Process:** 
1. Contact front desk or billing department
2. Provide receipt and explanation of dispute
3. Resolution typically within 7 business days
4. Refunds issued to original payment method

## Incidentals

**Room Service:** 18% service charge + 7% tax automatically added
**Mini-bar:** Charges post automatically within 15 minutes of removal
**Movies:** Charged immediately upon rental
**Late Checkout Fee:** Charged at checkout if not pre-authorized
