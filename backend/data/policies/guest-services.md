# Guest Services Policy - POL-001

## Check-in and Check-out

**Policy:** Standard check-in time is 3:00 PM, and check-out time is 12:00 PM noon.

**Early Check-in:** Available upon request, subject to room availability. No additional charge if room is ready before 1:00 PM.

**Late Check-out:** Complimentary late check-out until 2:00 PM for Bonvoy Titanium Elite and Ambassador Elite members. Other guests may request late check-out subject to a fee of $25 per hour.

## Room Changes

**Policy:** Guests may request a room change within the first 24 hours of stay at no charge if they are dissatisfied with their accommodated room.

**Upgrade Requests:** Upgrades are subject to availability and may be provided complimentary to elite members or for a fee based on the room category difference.

## Guest Complaints

**Response Time:** All guest complaints must be acknowledged within 15 minutes and resolved within 2 hours when possible.

**Compensation:** Associates are authorized to offer the following without management approval:
- Complimentary meal voucher up to $50
- Room service credit up to $75
- One free night on future stay (for serious issues)
- Immediate room upgrade (subject to availability)

## Special Requests

**Hypoallergenic Rooms:** Available upon advance request. Rooms are deep-cleaned and use special bedding.

**Accessibility:** ADA-compliant rooms available. Service animals are always welcome at no charge.

**Dietary Restrictions:** Kitchen can accommodate most dietary restrictions with 24 hours advance notice.
