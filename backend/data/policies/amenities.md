# Hotel Amenities Policy - POL-002

## Fitness Center

**Hours:** Open 24/7 for registered guests
**Access:** Room key required for entry
**Towel Service:** Complimentary towels available
**Personal Training:** Available by appointment, additional fee applies

## Pool and Spa

**Pool Hours:** 
- Main pool: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Hot tub: 6:00 AM - 11:00 PM

**Children:** Children under 16 must be supervised by an adult at all times.

**Cabanas:** Available for rent, $150 per day, includes bottled water and fruit plate.

**Spa Services:** Full spa menu available. Advance booking recommended. 20% gratuity automatically added to spa services.

## Business Center

**Hours:** 24/7 access with room key
**Services:** 
- Printing: First 10 pages free, $0.25 per page thereafter
- Faxing: $2 per page (domestic), $5 per page (international)
- Scanning: Complimentary
- Computers: Complimentary use for up to 2 hours per day

## Parking

**Self-Parking:** $25 per day
**Valet Parking:** $45 per day
**EV Charging:** Complimentary for Tesla and standard J1772 connectors
**Oversized Vehicles:** Additional $15 per day

## Pet Policy

**Allowed:** Dogs and cats only, maximum 2 pets per room
**Weight Limit:** Up to 50 lbs per pet
**Fee:** $150 per stay (non-refundable)
**Restrictions:** Pets may not be left unattended in rooms
**Amenities:** Pet beds, bowls, and treats provided upon request
